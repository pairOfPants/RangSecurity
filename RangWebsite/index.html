What is Rang?

Rang Cameras are an affordable security feature that have a built-in motion detection system that can access and capture your computer's camera when any motion is detected. When our app is active, it will be able to send you a text message informing you of the security breach at your computer and let you see it in real time. Additional features include the ability to start and stop the feed, capture a photo, and toggle our security mode. Every photo you take can be accessed via the cloud.

Our state-of-the-art motion capture system will use high resolution cameras to capture even the darkest of days with little to no compression. No condition is too extreme for this webcam!

This camera will capture a live feed of images, with a refresh rate of 120 frames per second, allowing for your computer to always be secure. The camera has a refresh rate that is 2x the average refresh rate, with leaps and bounds of better protection than our competitors.

Using the Twilio API, we send live refreshes on the security of your system, and any compromises will be sent immediately to your phone. You will have little to no delay between your computer being accessed and your notification.

Using secure storage, we make it so you are always able to see recordings of past days, or even weeks. You are constantly able to discern exactly who tried to access your computer, even weeks after the fact.
